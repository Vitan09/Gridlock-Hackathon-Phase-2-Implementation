# 🚦 GridLock AI – Event-Driven Traffic Congestion Forecasting & Smart Resource Management System

## Overview

GridLock AI is an AI-powered traffic intelligence platform designed to help city authorities proactively manage congestion caused by planned and unplanned events such as political rallies, festivals, sports events, road construction, accidents, VIP movements, and public gatherings.

Traditional traffic management systems are largely reactive and rely heavily on manual monitoring and operator experience. This often results in delayed responses, inefficient deployment of traffic personnel, poor diversion planning, and increased congestion. GridLock AI transforms this process into a proactive, data-driven system that predicts traffic impact, recommends operational resources, generates optimized diversion routes, and continuously improves through historical learning.

---

## The Problem

Urban traffic authorities face significant challenges when managing event-driven congestion:

* Traffic impact is rarely quantified before an event occurs.
* Resource deployment decisions are mostly experience-based.
* Diversion planning is often manual and time-consuming.
* There is no structured post-event learning mechanism.
* Authorities typically react after congestion develops rather than preventing it.

These challenges lead to increased travel times, fuel wastage, delayed emergency response, commuter dissatisfaction, and inefficient utilization of traffic management resources.

---

## Our Solution

GridLock AI provides a complete AI-powered decision support platform that enables traffic authorities to predict, plan, manage, and learn from traffic events.

The system combines machine learning, graph analytics, traffic network modeling, and continuous learning to support smarter operational decision-making.

### Key Capabilities

### 🤖 Traffic Impact Prediction

Predicts the probability that an event will cause severe congestion or road closures before the event occurs.

### 👮 Smart Resource Planning

Automatically recommends:

* Traffic police deployment
* Barricade requirements
* Alert levels
* Diversion strategies

based on predicted congestion severity.

### 🗺 Intelligent Diversion Routing

Generates optimized alternative routes using graph-based traffic network analysis to reduce congestion and improve traffic flow.

### 📈 Continuous Learning Engine

Learns from historical traffic outcomes to identify:

* High-risk zones
* High-risk junctions
* Resource utilization patterns
* Recurring congestion hotspots

and continuously improves future planning recommendations.
